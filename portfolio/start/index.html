Flight Track 247

For aviation enthusiasts.

Waiting for for my layover in many different airports, I started to grow an interest of where that Boeing 787 dreamliner or airbus A380 parked by gate 20 was going to.
With the combination of this interest and knowledge of programming, I decided to create Flight Track 247, a full stack web and mobile responsive application.

  • Developed using: JavaScript(es6), React, CSS3, HTML5, Bootstrap5, PostgreSQL, Node.js
  • Author: Andy Park

CarHealth+

Car's service and recall lists in one go

Cars play a crucial role in our lives. They take us anywhere we need to go at any given moment. Which is why it is so important to keep up with all services and recalls it needs to take you and your family anywhere safely. With CarHealth+, an AJAX project application, will give you all the service list it needs based on the mileage entered. You can also see what kind of recalls and complaints are out there from NHTSA bulletin board to ensure the safety of the driver and passengers.

  • Developed using: JavaScript (es5 & es6), CSS3, HTML5
  • Author: Andy Park

Planet Journal

Discovered a planet?

As our technology improves everyday, our ability to pierce through the uncharted territory of our universe advances as well. As a result, we find new planets at an increasing rate, which comes the question, "Are we alone in this universe?" Planet Journal is a simple application that allows user to save, edit, and delete information about the planet they've found.

  • Developed using: JavaScript (es5), CSS3, HTML5
  • Author: Andy Park
